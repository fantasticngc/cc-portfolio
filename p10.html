Description

Name: Shuyu Xu

Work Description:

Serveillance is everywhere which shows the power of collecting data from institutions, governments, and organizations. However, how the data looks like depends on the collectors and their intentions. If we collect data by ourselves, the results will be different from each other. The difference will demonstrate some issues and problems about socialogy, feminism, politics.

Design Process

I collect the data of female subject books from Open Library and I want to research how poeple are conscious of the rights of women and sexual equality.

First, I imported the data from Open Library API and searched the female subject and male subjects, using the two parts of data about released time and the book's number.

The first sketch is to compare the quantities of female subject books with the male subjects.





























The second is to show with time going, more and more female subjects books resease.





























The third one is about all the information that female and male subjects books, such as the authors, languages, and publishers.
